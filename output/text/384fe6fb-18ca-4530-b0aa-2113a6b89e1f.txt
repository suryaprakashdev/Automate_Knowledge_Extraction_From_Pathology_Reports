*** Diagnosis / Diagnoses: *** Resectate of portion of colon (sigmoid colon) with an ulcerated colon carcinoma conforming to the histological type of a moderately differentiated colorectal adenocarcinoma, extending no closer than 6 cm to the resection margin and with a greatest diameter of 3 cm. Invasive spread of tumor within all layers of the intestinal wall and as far as the neighboring mesocolic fatty tissue.. Remainder of the intestinal mucosal membrane with a small, tubular adenoma with mild epithelial dysplasia (synonym: low-grade intra-epithelial neoplasia). Oral and aboral resection margins tumor-free.. Twenty-two mesocolic lymph nodes with uncharacteristic reactive changes and tumor- free. Tumor stage thus pT3 pN0 (0/22) L0 V0; G2
